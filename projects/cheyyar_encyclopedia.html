Portfolio Detail

Developed "Cheyyar Encyclopedia," a user-friendly web application using Flask, HTML & CSS, and a spatial database to provide comprehensive information about Cheyyar town. Addressing the issue of limited accessible information, the app offers detailed insights into local amenities such as nearby villages, bus schedules, auto services, schools, colleges, hospitals, pharmacies, hotels, marriage halls, and supermarkets.

Integrated with Google Maps, the app facilitates easy navigation within the town. This project enhances convenience for residents and visitors by improving access to essential information and promoting a better understanding of local services and infrastructure. The use of a spatial database underscores the effectiveness of the application in meeting the informational needs of the community.
